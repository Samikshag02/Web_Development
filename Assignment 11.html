About Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the Indian national team in all formats. He is widely regarded as one of the best opening batsmen in the world and holds multiple records in international cricket, including the highest individual score in a One Day International (ODI) match. Known for his elegant style of batting, he has also been a key player for the Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League (IPL).
